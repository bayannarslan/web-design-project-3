Description:

Tale of the fanciful adventures of a ten-year-old girl named Chihiro, who discovers a secret world when she and her family get lost and venture through a hillside tunnel. When her parents undergo a mysterious transformation, Chihiro must fend for herself as she encounters strange spirits, assorted creatures and a grumpy sorceress who seeks to prevent her from returning to the human world.


Movie Info:

  • A 2001 Japanese Animated film written and directed by well known directer, Hayao Miyazaki, and animated by Studio Ghibli.

My Review:

Spirited Away is one of my favorite movies because of how unique the storyline is and how beautifully drawn and animated it is. The movie has so many details in the background, like Miyazaki's other works, and it feels like it is drawn with so much attention. Spirited Away is such a visually stunning film filled with some of the most imaginative characters I've ever seen. In addition to the visuals, the music is beautiful, and the story has something for audience members of all ages.
